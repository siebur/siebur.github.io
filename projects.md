# [Project portfolios](./projectsp.html)

"Managing a project portfolio is not just running multiple projects simultaneously. Each portfolio of projects must be analyzed individually, seeking to identify its capacity to generate value for the business and its adherence to the objectives defined in the strategic planning. In the portfolio, there should be one or more well defined business objectives and goals tangible benefits.  We have models of projects in development and cases of success.

Project Portfolio Management (PPM) works on a number of projects in a single portfolio, with common result objectives. It aims to maximize benefits and optimize the integrated allocation of enterprise resources.

The main objective of portfolio management is to optimize return on investment (ROI) applied throughout the projects and in full harmony with the strategic goals and articulations of the enterprise."
